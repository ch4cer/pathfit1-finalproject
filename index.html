What is Exercise?

Exercise is any purposeful and structured bodily activity undertaken to maintain or improve physical fitness, overall health, and general wellness. It encompasses a wide spectrum of physical movements, from daily activities like walking and cycling to intensive, scheduled routines like weightlifting, running, and specialized sports training.
